

## Happy 3rd Year Anniversary Website 💛

### Design & Theme
- **Color palette**: Warm gold (#D4A017, #F5C518) with cream/ivory backgrounds and soft dark text
- **Typography**: Elegant serif font for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Overall feel**: Romantic, warm, and nostalgic

### Page 1 — Opening
- Full-screen warm gold gradient background
- Centered text: *"Ready to Travel Back 3 Years Ago?"*
- Animated "Begin" button that slides to the next page

### Page 2 — Year 1 (March 2023 – February 2024)
- **Vertical video** plays looped in the background, filling the screen (styled for portrait/mobile-first ratio)
- Semi-transparent overlay for readability
- Month-by-month sections with month/year header
- **5 photos per month in a grid collage** layout overlaid on the video
- Swipe/slide transition to navigate to the next page

### Page 3 — Year 2 (March 2024 – February 2025)
- Same layout as Page 2, same background video continues
- Month-by-month grid collage for Year 2

### Page 4 — Year 3 (March 2025 – March 2026)
- Same layout, same background video
- Month-by-month grid collage for Year 3

### Page 5 — Love Letter
- Same background video
- Elegant letter card centered on screen with decorative **lily and peony** illustrations (SVG/CSS art) framing the letter
- Blank editable text area for you to add your personal message
- Warm gold accents and floral borders

### Navigation
- Smooth **slide transitions** between pages (left/right swipe or arrow buttons)
- Subtle page indicator dots
- Each year page scrolls vertically through the months, then slides horizontally to the next page

### Placeholder Setup
- All photos will use placeholder images — you can upload and replace them later
- Video will use a placeholder — you'll drop in your edited vertical video
